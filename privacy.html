What is GDPR?

The General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) is a regulation that applies to all countries within the EU/EEA. It replaces previous national privacy laws and aims to harmonise data protection across Europe. It ensures stronger protection of personal data and gives individuals more control over how their information is used.

Under GDPR, individuals have the right to request that their personal data be deleted when it is no longer necessary, provided the company is not required to retain it for legal purposes.
