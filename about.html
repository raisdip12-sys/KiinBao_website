Meet the Owners

Addy and Henrik

Addy and Henrik are the driving force behind Kiin Kiin Bao Bao and the wider Kiin restaurant family, built on a shared passion for exceptional hospitality and culinary innovation.

Through their vision and dedication, Kiin Kiin achieved the remarkable distinction of earning two Michelin stars, establishing itself as one of the most celebrated dining experiences in the Nordic culinary scene.

Known for their eye for talent, Addy and Henrik have consistently brought together outstanding chefs and creative culinary teams along size amazing service, empowering them to express their artistry and elevate every concept they build.

Across each of their restaurants, their vision remains the same — to create unforgettable dining experiences where creativity, quality, and atmosphere meet at the highest level.
